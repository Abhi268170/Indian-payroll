## Form 16 Structure (Statutory)

Form 16 has two parts:

### Part A
- Downloaded from TRACES (government portal) after Form 24Q is filed
- Contains: TDS certificate number, employer TAN, employee PAN, quarterly TDS breakup
- Cannot be generated by Zoho — must be downloaded from TRACES and uploaded to Zoho

### Part B
- Generated by Zoho Payroll
- Contains: Salary breakup, deductions under Chapter VI-A, tax computation, net taxable income
- Employer must sign Part B and issue to employee by 15th June (after financial year end)

---

## Form 16 Generation Process (4 Steps Visible in UI)

### Step 1: Upload Part A
- Source: Download from TRACES portal (employer logs in with TAN credentials)
- File format: PDF (digitally signed by TRACES)
- Upload: Per employee, matching via PAN
- Status indicator: Shows count of employees with Part A uploaded

### Step 2: Generate
- Zoho generates Part B for all employees
- Merges Part A (uploaded) with Part B (generated) into combined Form 16 PDF
- Pre-condition: Form 24Q must be filed for all 4 quarters of the financial year
- Pre-condition: Tax Deductor configured with TAN

### Step 3: Sign
- Form 16 requires digital signature of the authorized signatory
- Options: DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) or manual signature on printed copy
- Zoho may support DSC-based signing or generate unsigned PDFs for manual signing

### Step 4: Publish / Email
- Publish: Makes Form 16 available in the employee self-service portal
- Email: Sends Form 16 PDF to employee's email address
- Bulk action: All employees in one click, or individual employee

## Form 16 Statutory Requirements

| Requirement | Statutory Reference |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Issue deadline | 15th June after FY end (e.g., 15th June 2027 for FY2026-27) |
| Mandatory for salary > basic exemption | Section 203 |
| Must bear TAN of employer | Rule 31 |
| Part A from TRACES, Part B from employer | Rule 31(3) |
| Employee PAN required | Without PAN: TDS at 20% flat rate |
| Digital signature or manual sign | Rule 31(1A) allows DSC |

## Current System Gap

Since Tax Deductor is not configured:
1. TAN is missing → Form 24Q text file will be invalid
2. Form 16 generation blocked
3. TDS deduction continues but is orphaned (no deductor to attribute it to)
