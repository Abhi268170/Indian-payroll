## Summary of Key Findings

### Architecture
- Zoho splits Form 16 into Part A (TRACES-downloaded) and Part B (Zoho-generated)
- Part A is NOT generated by Zoho — it must be downloaded from TRACES and uploaded
- Zoho merges Part A + Part B into a single PDF per employee
- No direct TRACES API integration observed

### Prerequisites (Gate Checks)
| Gate | Status in Test Org |
|------|-------------------|
| Tax Deductor configured (TAN + PAN + Deductor name) | BLOCKED — not found |
| Form 24Q filed for all quarters | BLOCKED — not filed |
| Part A downloaded from TRACES | Dependent on Form 24Q |

### Generation Flow
4 steps: Upload Part A → Generate → Sign → Publish/Email

### Digital Signature
- DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) — hardware token
- e-Sign (Aadhaar-based) — Aadhaar OTP authentication
- Unsigned distribution also permitted

### Employee Access
- Post-publish: visible in Employee Self-Service Portal under Payslips & Forms tab
- Password-protected PDF (PAN as password)
- Can also be emailed directly

### Statutory Deadline
- Rule 31: Form 16 must be issued by 31 May after end of financial year
- FY2025-26 → deadline: 31/05/2026
