# Compliance > Form 16 — Part A

## URL / Navigation Path
- Taxes & Forms > Form 16: `#/taxes-and-forms/form16`
- (Same as Form 16 module: `#/taxes-and-forms/form16?fiscal_year=2026`)

## Purpose
Part A of Form 16 is issued by the employer (tax deductor) to the employee (deductee). It contains the TAN of the employer, the PAN of the employee, and quarter-wise TDS deposited with the government. Part A is downloaded from TRACES after the quarterly Form 24Q is filed and accepted by TRACES.

## Zoho's Approach to Part A
**Zoho does not generate Part A internally.** Part A is downloaded from TRACES (Tax Deductor and Collector Registration/Compliance and Reporting System) by the employer after Form 24Q is processed. Zoho's Form 16 workflow requires:
1. Download Part A from TRACES (external step)
2. Upload Part A to Zoho
3. Zoho merges Part A (from TRACES) with Part B (generated by Zoho) to produce a consolidated Form 16 PDF

This is confirmed by the Form 16 landing page 4-step flow:
> Upload Form 16 Part A → Generate Form 16 → Sign Form 16 → Publish/Email

## Part A Data Fields (as specified by TRACES format)

When Part A is downloaded from TRACES, it contains:

| Field | Source |
|-------|--------|
| Employer TAN | From Tax Details settings |
| Employer PAN | From Tax Details settings |
| Employer Name | From Org Profile |
| Employee PAN | From Employee Personal Information |
| Employee Name | From Employee profile |
| Assessment Year | Derived from financial year |
| Quarter-wise challan details | From Form 24Q filed with TRACES |
| Quarter-wise TDS deposited | From Form 24Q accepted by TRACES |
| BSR Code | From challan payment |
| Challan date | From challan payment |
| Challan serial number | From challan payment |
| TDS deposited per quarter | From TRACES records |

## Upload Part A Flow
1. Navigate to Taxes & Forms > Form 16
2. Select Financial Year from dropdown
3. "Upload Form 16 Part A" step — upload the TRACES-downloaded file
4. System matches Part A data to employees by PAN
5. Merged Part A + Part B form is created per employee

## Preconditions for Part A Upload
- Form 24Q must be filed with TRACES for all quarters in the FY
- TRACES must have processed and accepted Form 24Q
- Part A certificate downloaded from TRACES (employer login)
- Tax Deductor must be configured in Zoho settings

## Government Portal Integration (TRACES)
- **TRACES is the source of Part A** — not generated by Zoho
- Employer downloads Part A from TRACES: https://www.tdscpc.gov.in
- Format: digitally signed PDF (Form 16 Part A certificate)
- No API integration with TRACES observed in Zoho
- Entire Part A workflow is external to Zoho; Zoho only handles upload + merge

## Statutory Rules Referenced
- Section 203 (certificate of TDS — Form 16 obligation on employer)
- Rule 31 (due date for Part A issuance: 31 May after end of FY)
- Rule 31A (Form 24Q filing — prerequisite for TRACES Part A availability)
- Part A is a TRACES-generated document; employer cannot self-generate it

## Cross-Module Dependencies
- Settings > Tax Details (TAN, PAN, Deductor details — MANDATORY)
- Taxes & Forms > Form 24Q (must be filed for all 4 quarters before Part A is available on TRACES)
- Taxes & Forms > Challans (challan details feed into Form 24Q)
- Employee Personal Information (PAN — must exist per employee)
- Reports > Taxes and Forms > Form 24Q report

## Key Observations for Our Build
1. **Part A is NOT generated by our system** — we must build an upload mechanism to accept TRACES-downloaded Part A files (PDF or XML). Match by employee PAN.
2. **Tax Deductor gate is critical** — our Form 16 generation should hard-block if TAN/PAN/Deductor are not configured.
3. **"Cannot change deductor details after generation"** — this is an invariant: once Form 16 generation starts for a FY, the deductor entity becomes immutable. Build this lock into the state machine.
4. **Part A is digitally signed by TRACES** — when we merge, we must not alter the Part A content. Only combine as composite PDF.
5. **FY selector** — Form 16 is annual, not quarterly. Our UI should filter by financial year, not calendar year.
6. **Assessment Year** = Financial Year + 1 (e.g., FY2025-26 → AY2026-27).
